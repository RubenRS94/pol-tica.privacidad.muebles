03

Purposes and legal basis

We process your data for the following purposes and on the following legal grounds:

Purpose Legal basis
Order management and delivery — processing your purchase, managing shipping and invoicing Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)
User account management — creating and maintaining your profile in the shop Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR)

04

Data retention

We retain your data only for as long as is strictly necessary for the purpose for which it was collected and to comply with applicable legal obligations:

  • Customer and order data: 5 years from the last transaction, as required by tax and commercial law.
  • Newsletter data: until you unsubscribe or withdraw your consent.
  • Contact form data: 1 year from resolution of the enquiry.
  • Browsing data (analytics cookies): maximum 13 months.
  • Inactive user accounts: 3 years from the last activity.

Once these periods expire, your data will be securely deleted or anonymised.

05

Recipients and disclosures

We do not sell or transfer your personal data to third parties for their own commercial purposes. However, in order to provide our service, we share data with the following trusted providers acting as data processors:

  • Shipping and logistics companies — to manage order delivery (name, address, phone number).
  • Payment gateway — to process payments securely (card data handled entirely by them under PCI-DSS standards).
  • Email and CRM providers — to send order confirmations and newsletters.
  • Web analytics platform — for anonymous or pseudonymised usage statistics.
  • Web hosting services — technical infrastructure on which the shop operates.

All our providers are bound by data processing agreements that ensure GDPR compliance. We may also share data with public authorities when required to do so by law.

06

International transfers

As a general rule, your data is processed within the European Economic Area (EEA). In the exceptional cases where it is necessary to transfer data to countries outside the EEA, we ensure that adequate safeguards are in place through:

  • Adequacy decisions by the European Commission.
  • Standard contractual clauses approved by the European Commission.
  • Other mechanisms recognised under the GDPR (Art. 46).

You can request information about the applicable safeguards by writing to us at privacy@naturahome.es.

07

Your rights

As a data subject, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

Access

Obtain confirmation as to whether we process your data and access a copy of it.

Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data relating to you.

Erasure

Request deletion of your data when it is no longer necessary or you withdraw consent.

Objection

Object to processing based on legitimate interests or carried out for direct marketing purposes.

Restriction

Request that we restrict processing in certain circumstances provided for by law.

Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format to transfer it to another provider.

Withdrawal of consent

Revoke any consent given at any time, without retroactive effect.

Complaint to the supervisory authority

Lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority if you believe we have infringed your rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please send an email to privacy@naturahome.es or a letter to our postal address, stating your full name, a copy of your identity document and the right you wish to exercise. We will respond within a maximum of 30 days.

08

Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our site. We use the following types:

  • Strictly necessary cookies: essential for the shop to function (shopping cart, user session). No consent required.
  • Preference cookies: remember your settings (language, region). Consent required.
  • Analytics cookies: help us understand how you use the site so we can improve it (pages visited, session duration). Consent required.
  • Marketing cookies: used to show you relevant advertising on other sites. Consent required.

You can manage or withdraw your cookie consent at any time through the cookie preferences panel available at the bottom of the website, or by configuring your browser to block them. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect the functioning of the shop.

For more information, please see our Cookie policy.

09

Minors

Natura Home's services are intended for persons aged 16 or over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under that age. If you are under 16, you need the consent of your parent or legal guardian to register or make a purchase.

If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor without the required parental consent, we will delete it immediately. If you are aware of such a situation, please notify us at privacy@naturahome.es.

10

Data security

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction or alteration. These include, among others:

  • Encrypted communications via HTTPS/TLS protocol.
  • Access to data restricted to authorised personnel with an operational need.
  • Payment gateway certified to PCI-DSS standard — we do not store card data on our servers.
  • Regular security reviews and system updates.
  • Incident response plans and notification to the supervisory authority in the event of a data breach where required by law.

However, no transmission of data over the Internet is completely secure. If you suspect misuse of your data, please contact us immediately.

  • A prominent notice on the website before the changes take effect.
  • An email if you are a registered customer or newsletter subscriber.

The "last updated" date shown at the top of this document always reflects the current version. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
